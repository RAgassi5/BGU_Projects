How to Play Race To The Finals
==============================

Board Markers
-------------
• **Start position**
  Your player icon appears here at the beginning of each maze.

• **Goal position**
  The NBA Finals trophy marks the exit you must reach.

• **Solution path**
  A trail of basketballs shows the computed shortest path from start to goal.

Controls
--------
• **Arrow keys (← ↑ → ↓)** – move left, up, right, down.
• **Numeric keypad (4 8 6 2)** – move left, up, right, down.
• **Diagonal moves (1 7 9 3)** – move down-left, up-left, up-right, down-right.
• **Mouse drag** – click and drag your player icon to any reachable cell.

Tools & Buttons
---------------
• **LeBron James icon** – your avatar in the maze.

• **Solve** (in-game) – display the solution path as basketballs.

• **Save** (in-game) – write the current maze (and your progress) to a file.

• **New Game** (Main Menu) – generate a fresh maze with custom dimensions.
        **New**: start a brand-new maze.
        **Load**: open a previously saved maze file.

• **Options** (Main Menu) – view and edit application properties.

• **About** (Main Menu) – see information about this application.

• **Exit** (Main Menu) – quit the game and shut down background servers.

Tips & Tricks
-------------
• You can mix keyboard and mouse moves freely.
• If you get stuck, click **Show Solution** to display the solution route!

Logging
-------
• Every time you start a **New Game**, a record is appended to **logs/generate.log**, detailing the client address, maze dimensions, and timestamp.
• Every time you click **Show Solution**, a record is appended to **logs/solve.log**, including a prompt saying the player asked for a solution and timestamp.
